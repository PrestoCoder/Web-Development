- Strongly typed vs Weakly typed
Strongly typed means all variables have specific types, and are stored inflexibly in the amount of space that is assigned to that variable.
JS is less strongly typed, or say weakly typed, as all different types of values don't require different types of variables.
JS requires browser to run.

- ECMA Script (ES) is the version name of JS. ES-5 is compatible with most browsers.
